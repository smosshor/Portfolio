INSIGHTS

INSIGHT 1

The main goal of animal rescue is to educate, not confiscate.

INSIGHT 2

Laws are complicated and don’t match people’s assumptions.

INSIGHT 3

Misreporting can have serious consequences for the organization, people, and animals involved.

INSIGHT 4

Animal rescue operations are collaborative and co-dependent.

INSIGHT 5

Animal rescue is like social work; it involves people, not just animals.

INSIGHT 6

For citizen reporters, lack of feedback after reporting is frustrating and disheartening.

Prototyping

We went through three cycles of prototyping, user testing and iteration before designing our final MVP; lo-fidelity storyboards, mid-fidelity wireframes, and high-fidelity interactive HTML/ CSS prototype.

Prototyping

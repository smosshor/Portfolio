Weather Dashboard Interaction Design Overview, Fall 2014

Project: Weather Dashboard

  • Prompt: Design a weather dashboard that includes information about past, present and future weather trends and is specific to a location.
  • Interpretting weather forecasts can be difficult because it's hard to really "feel" what the difference between 40 and 50 degrees is. With this in mind, I designed this dashboard to support users in comparing the forecasted temperatures with recent past temperatures, so they know if it's getting colder or warming up.

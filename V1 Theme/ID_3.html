Open DRI Instructional Design

At TechChange, I supported the build and QA of a course for the Open Data for Resilience Initiative, Open DRI, a World Bank project that works to apply the concepts of the global open data movement to the challenges of reducing vulnerability to natural hazards and the impacts of climate change.

Project Details

  • Conducted extensive Quality Assurance (QA), fixing bugs and design issues in Articulate.
  • Refined design of text-heavy and confusing components to optimize usability and learner experience.
  • Added extensive javascript trigger to capture key data for research and analysis.

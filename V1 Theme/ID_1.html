Best Practices of Teaching Online Instructional Design

As an independent project during my Instructional Design Fellowship at TechChange, I created a self-paced online course on "The Best Practices of Teaching Online". The course is designed for TechChange clients to improve both the educational and visual design of their online courses, as well as for internal use.

Project Details

  • Collaboratively mapped out key learning objective with TechChange staff, referencing educational science principles and research.
  • Created custom interactive activities and content with Adobe Illustrator and Articulate Storyline

hello!

I'm a digital designer, developer and researcher currently working with CrowdRise, part of the incredible GoFundMe family. I get to help awesome non-profit organizations from the Red Cross to American Cancer Society to your local non-profit animal shelters.

I recently graduated from Carnegie Mellon University with majors in Human Computer Interaction, Decision Science and a minor in Learning Media. I really like designing things that encourage people to make better decisions, do more good in the world, and live healthier lives. I believe in the power of user-centered design to bridge the gap between real problems and imagined solutions.
